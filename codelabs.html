Hey!This is Prantik Chatterjee, the founder of The CodeLabs Project.A self taught developer still in the university on the verge of graduating.CodeLabs is a independent open source project developed by me to give you all the free but best resources available on the internet.

Connect With Me

Now that you are clear with my intro, let's come to the CodeLabs part.

You want to learn yourself.That's Awesome!It's time consuming and difficult to stay motivated. Above all, you usually don't even know if you're learning the right stuff or not.We lead you to the best free resources available online.


It is hard to know where to begin.That's where we come in.Learn on your own with the curated resources provided by CodeLabs.To be absolutely true it's hard! It takes about 800 hours of work to go from zero to employable as a web developer. The project aims to solve the biggest issues for a self learner - know what to learn, when to learn and how to learn.Enjoy the world of coding.

The project as said is entirely open sourced and you are free to make any suggestion with us.We would love to hear back from you. You can even fork our Github repository and help us to improve CodeLabs.
